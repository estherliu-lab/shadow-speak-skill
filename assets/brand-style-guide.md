# Brand Style Guide

## Keywords

温柔、实用、日系手账、轻学习、不压迫、可复制、可打卡、可分享、随身日语嘴替、温柔但专业的日语教练。

## Colors

- Cream: #FAF7F0
- Card: #FFFDF8
- Matcha: #7F9B76
- Deep Matcha: #5F7F58
- Beige: #D8C3A5
- Soft Orange: #E8A87C
- Text: #2F2F2F
- Muted: #7A746B

## UI Temperament

Quiet, useful, warm, app-like, suitable for screenshots. Avoid exam pressure and cold AI dashboard feelings.

## Do Not Use

Cold AI tool style, question-bank style, heavy gradients, large purple palette, childish cartoons, excessive animation, or decorative text blocks that do not help learning.
